Fantasy dungeon keeper
Artstyle: Top down pixel art or low poly
1. Keep prisoners(fantasy mosnters like slimes etc.) from escaping(mostly by clicking on jail doors to repair them and dragging them back into cells) -Core
2. Torture them for information(quick time events or something) -2nd
3. Gather a set amount of information -Progression
4. Share information willingly with someone that has power over you or get hurt(or killed). -Narrative
